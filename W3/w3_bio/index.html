BIOGRAPHY

Hello, my name is Andrew Castro! I was born and raised in Rhode Island. However, my dad is from Guatemala. My mother was born and raised and Rhode Island as well. But, her parents (my grandparents) are from Portugual (São Miguel). Ethnically I am Portuguese, Filipino, Spanish, and "technically" Chinese. But I think that would be too distant to count at this point. As for languages- I can only fluently speak in English. However, I can understand the majority of what my family says in Portuguese, specifically their dialect. I can fully read and write in Korean/한글, but my vocabulary and grammar are too elementary to be considered anywhere near fluent...

I started going to New England Tech in Fall 2021 after attending CCRI for about ~2 years, and getting some cheaper community college credits... Which ended up being more expensive in the long run... I was originally majoring in Cyber Security with a focus on Networking but I found it dreadfully boring. I love technology and the depths it can go in, but I realized it's MUCH closer to the creative side of it. (i.e Programming)

Well... That's all for my bio page. Bye!
