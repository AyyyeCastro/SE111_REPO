REVIEW

Andrew Castro 2022.01.31



This review will contain potential spoilers. Please refrain from reading if this may effect you.

Final Fantasy XIV is a movie about the Seven Scions of The Seventh Dawn in the world of Eorzea. Starting from A Realm Reborn (the initial movie), we are introduced to the Mother Crystal. We do not yet know the Mother Crystal's identity, or her intentions... We can only Hear, Feel, and Think about it. The very existance of the Mother Crystal is yet a further mystery the Scions of the Seventh Dawn must reveal within Final Fantasy XIV.

The Scions of the Seventh Dawn in Final Fantasy XIV are first met with danger as they encounter an Ascian, who has taken over their very friend, Thancred. As they dig deeper into the secrets, they figure out that Garlemald and the Ascians may be linked together in some way.

The script of Final Fantasy XIV is an incredibly well written story, and the further you dive into the story, the more you realize that nothing is what it may seem. You can read more about FFXIV on IMDB.
